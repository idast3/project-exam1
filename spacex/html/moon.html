MOON FLY-BY

For the first time in history, SpaceX will take a group of private passengers on a fly-by of the moon. The mission is planned to happen in 2023. It will take about a week for the passengers to fly around the Moon and back to Earth.

So far only 24 people have been to the Moon, and this will be the first mission since Apollo 17 in 1972.

THE FIRST PRIVATE PASSENGER

Yusaku Maezaw, a Japanese billionaire, art curator and founder of the online retailer Zozotown, will be the first private passenger to take a holiday trip around the Moon. He plans to bring along a party of fellow artists to be part of his trip and art project #dearMoon.

Maezaw is helping to fund the development of the of SpaceX’s Starship and Super Heavy rocket. This will bring us one big step further in fulfilling the dreams of everyday people who wants to travel to space.

Maezaw has an art project called #dearMoon.
