BUILDING LIFE ON MARS

We are preparing to send the very first people to Mars already in 2024. A cargo mission will be launched two years ahead to prepare the crew with water resources, power, mining and life support infrastructure.

The ships from this mission will be used to set up the beginnings of a base where our ultimate goal is to build a city with a self-sustaining civilization on Mars.

The crew currently in training for the mission are prepared for this as a one-way trip, but we hope to build technology that will make an Earth return possible in the future.

SUPER HEAVY + STARSHIP

Super Heavy (rocket) and Starship (spaceship) will be designed for missions around the Earth, Moon and Mars as a fully reusable transportation system.

The Starship will be used for satellites, space station missions and interplanetary missions.
